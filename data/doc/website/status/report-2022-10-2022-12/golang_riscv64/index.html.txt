Contact: Mikaël Urankar <mikael@FreeBSD.org>
Contact: Dmitri Goutnik <dmgk@FreeBSD.org>

the patches merged. golang on FreeBSD riscv64 will be available in
golang v1.20 (to be released in February 2023).
