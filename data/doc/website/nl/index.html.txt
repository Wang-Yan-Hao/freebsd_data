The FreeBSD Project

FreeBSD is an operating system used to power modern servers, desktops,
and embedded platforms. A large community has continually developed it
for more than thirty years. Its advanced networking, security, and
storage features have made FreeBSD the platform of choice for many of
the busiest web sites and most pervasive embedded networking and storage
devices.
