FreeBSD Past Events

Please send details of any FreeBSD related events or events that are of
interest for FreeBSD users which are not listed here to
freebsd-doc@FreeBSD.org.

Countries and regions displayed in dark red on the map below are hosting
upcoming FreeBSD-related events. Countries that have hosted
FreeBSD-related events in the past show up in yellow or orange, with
darker colors representing a larger number of previous events.

[events map]

Past Events:

December 2022

-   An Introduction to FreeBSD Services – Drew Gurkowski, FreeBSD
    Fridays – The FreeBSD Foundation, ,
    16 - 16 December, 2022
    Setting up printing, audio, and Wi-Fi on a fresh FreeBSD install.

November 2022

-   November 2022 FreeBSD Vendor Summit, Online, ,
    03 - 04 November, 2022
    Join us for the online November 2022FreeBSD Vendor Summit. The event
    will consist of virtual, half day sessions, taking place November
    3-4, 2022. It’s free to attend, but we ask that you register with
    the eventbrite system to gain access to the meeting room and
    separate hallway track. The vendor summit is sponsored by the
    FreeBSD Foundation.

October 2022

-   Aberdeen Hackathon 2022, 57 North Hacklab, Aberdeen, Scotland
    08 - 08 October, 2022
    A FreeBSD Hackathon will take place in Aberdeen, Scotland from the
    5th of October to the 8th of October 2022. The hackathon will be
    hosted by 57 North Hacklab.

September 2022

-   EuroBSDcon 2022, The Freihaus Campus of Vienna's Technical
    University, Vienna, Austria
    15 - 18 September, 2022
    EuroBSDcon is the European annual technical conference gathering
    users and developers working on and with 4.4BSD (Berkeley Software
    Distribution) based operating systems family and related projects.

June 2022

-   June 2022 FreeBSD Developer Summit, Online, ,
    16 - 17 June, 2022
    Join us for the online June 2022 FreeBSD Developer Summit. The event
    will consist of virtual, half day sessions, taking place June
    16-17, 2022. It is free to attend, but we ask that you register with
    the conference system to gain access to the meeting room (more
    information to come). In addition to developer discussion sessions,
    we will also have vendor talks.

-   BSDCan 2022, Online, ,
    01 - 04 June, 2022
    A four day BSD conference held online. BSDCan hosts talks and
    tutorials on a range of topics based around the BSD family of
    operating systems.

March 2022

-   Google Summer of Code, FreeBSD Office Hours – YouTube, ,
    16 - 16 March, 2022
    The then upcoming Google Summer of Code, and how mentees could
    become involved.

February 2022

-   How to Track FreeBSD Using Git, Part 2 – Warner Losh, FreeBSD
    Fridays – YouTube, ,
    11 - 11 February, 2022
    A primer on how to track FreeBSD using Git. The basics of obtaining
    and compiling FreeBSD, then more advanced topics – including
    managing local changes, and how to contribute those changes to the
    FreeBSD Project.

-   FOSDEM 2022, Online, ,
    05 - 06 February, 2022
    Every year, thousands of developers of free and open source software
    from all over the world gather at the event in Brussels. In 2022,
    they will gather online. Be sure to stop by the FreeBSD stand and
    BSD devroom to get the latest on FreeBSD.
